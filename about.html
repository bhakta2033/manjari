Nepal, an Asian country, is located along the southern slopes of the Himalayas.
It is a landlocked country located between India to the east, south and west and Tibet Autonomous Region of China to the north.
Its territory stretches about 500 miles (800 km) from east to west and 90 to 150 miles from north to south.
The capital is Kathmandu. Himalayas in northern Nepal.
